Biography

Associate Professor, University of Oviedo, Spain. Founder and main researcher of WESO (Web Semantics Oviedo) research group, which collaborates with different companies around the world applying semantic web technologies to solve practical problems.

Member of the W3C Web of Things Working Group and past member of the W3C Data Shapes Working Group.

Member of : Shape Expressions, SHACL and RAX (RDF and XML interoperability). Chairman of the Best practices of Multilingual Linked Open Data W3c community group.

Implemented the SHACLex library and maintains an online RDF validator service.

He is the coordinator of the Master in Web Engineering - University of Oviedo.

He was the Dean of the School of Computer Science Engineering - University of Oviedo from 2004 to 2012.
